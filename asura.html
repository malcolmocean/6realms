The asuras have nearly everything the gods have — power, splendor, capability. The old image is exact: a wish-fulfilling tree grows with its roots in the asuras' realm, but its fruit ripens in the realm of the gods above. So they live mid-lunge, forever measuring the distance to somebody else's garden. This is the realm of the scoreboard: nothing is ever an achievement, only a ranking.
