# the six realms

A small field guide to the six realms of samsara — hell, hungry ghost, animal, human, titan, god — treated the way the psychological reading of the bhavachakra treats them: not as places you go after death but as weathers the mind moves through, each with its own inner logic, its own trap, and its own door.

Rather than explaining the realms in our own words, the site tries to give **texture**: quotes from Buddhist teachers (Chögyam Trungpa, Patrul Rinpoche, *Opening the Heart of Compassion*), and poems, films, songs, and public-domain paintings that were made from inside each realm — sometimes on purpose, sometimes not. Art via Wikimedia Commons (Bosch, the Gaki-zōshi scrolls, Bruegel, Dürer, Cornelis van Haarlem, Tiepolo, the Wellcome Collection's wheel-of-life thangka); each page's palette and type are keyed to its painting.

Plain HTML + one stylesheet, no build step.

Live at [malcolmocean.github.io/6realms](https://malcolmocean.github.io/6realms/).

May all beings be free.
