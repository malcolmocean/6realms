# the six realms

A small field guide to the six realms of samsara — hell, hungry ghost, animal, human, jealous god, god — treated the way the psychological reading of the bhavachakra treats them: not as places you go after death but as weathers the mind moves through, each with its own inner logic, its own trap, and its own door.

Rather than explaining the realms in our own words, the site tries to give **texture**: quotes from Buddhist teachers (Chögyam Trungpa, Patrul Rinpoche, *Opening the Heart of Compassion*), and poems, films, and songs that were written from inside each realm — sometimes on purpose, sometimes not.

Plain HTML + one stylesheet, no build step. Each realm page carries its own weather (palette/mood) inside one shared typographic voice.

Live at [malcolmocean.github.io/6realms](https://malcolmocean.github.io/6realms/).

May all beings be free.
